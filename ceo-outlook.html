Talent

A resilient workforce and targeted reskilling initiatives will determine which organisations adapt quickly. CEOs plan to invest in talent strategies that balance technical skills and leadership agility. Building diverse teams with systems thinking is central to accelerating impact across markets.

ESG

ESG remains strategic: customers and partners expect verifiable sustainability outcomes. Companies that integrate ESG into product design and operations benefit from market trust and long-term growth.

Foreword

As we celebrate a year of our One Africa journey, I am delighted to introduce the second edition of the Stella Lumen CEO Outlook. This edition reflects conversations with leaders across sectors who are doubling down on technology, talent and purpose to build resilient organisations.
